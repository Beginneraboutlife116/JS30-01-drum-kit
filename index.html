Welcome! This is a drum kit game.

You could just press the correspond keyboard to make sound. You could also use the 箭頭上、箭頭右 to select the forward key or use the 箭頭下、箭頭左 to select the backward key, and after that using Space or Enter to click and make sound. Just feel free to test it 😉
